HTML Paragraphs HTML paragraphs are a fundamental element for organizing and displaying textual content on web pages. By using them correctly and adhering to best practices, you can enhance the structure, readability, and accessibility of your web content. The p tag defines a paragraph in HTML. A paragraph always starts on a new line, and browsers automatically add some white space (a margin) before and after a paragraph. HTML paragraphs adapt to different screen sizes and resized windows. You cannot change their display by adding extra spaces or lines in your HTML code; browsers automatically remove any extra spaces and lines.
